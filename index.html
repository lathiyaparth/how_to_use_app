What is the concept of LL?

LL is a service-based company. It provides various types of services including meetings, notes, transactions, and complaints. LL aims to save both your money and time.

LL aims to provide a better experience, and its main focus is on how to save more money and time for consumers. LL is an open-source project and provides end-to-end security.

LL's official partners include Zoom Meetings, Firebase, and Zego Cloud.
